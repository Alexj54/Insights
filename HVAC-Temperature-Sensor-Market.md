HVAC Temperature Sensor Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2029

The global HVAC Temperature Sensor market was valued at US$ 754.4 million in 2022 and is projected to reach US$ 1066.7 million by 2029, at a CAGR of 5.1% during the forecast period. The influence of COVID-19 and the Russia-Ukraine War were considered while estimating market sizes.

A temperature sensor is a device that detects and measures hotness and coolness and converts it into an electrical signal. HVAC temperature sensors provide temperature measurement, control and compensation. HVAC temperature sensors allow technicians to easily monitor and adjust conditions to ensure the safety and comfort of the building's inhabitants. HVAC temperature sensors include: NTC thermistors, PTC thermistors, RTDs, thermocouples etc.

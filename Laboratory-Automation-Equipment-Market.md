Laboratory Automation Equipment Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2023-2029

The global Laboratory Automation Equipment market was valued at US$ 12940 million in 2022 and is projected to reach US$ 19750 million by 2029, at a CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period. The influence of COVID-19 and the Russia-Ukraine War were considered while estimating market sizes.

The main types of Laboratory Automation Equipment in this report are Task Targeted Automation (TTA), Laboratory Automation (TLA) and Separate Automation Equipment.

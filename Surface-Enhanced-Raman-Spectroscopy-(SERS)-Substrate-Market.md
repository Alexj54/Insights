The Surface-Enhanced Raman Spectroscopy (SERS) substrates enhances the Raman scattering light from molecules, making high-sensitive Raman spectroscopic analysis possible. Typical Surface-Enhanced Raman Spectroscopy (SERS) Substrates are roughened silver/copper/gold surfaces. The SERS technique requires adsorption of the analyte molecules onto the Surface-Enhanced Raman Spectroscopy (SERS) Substrate.
